Our Services

Comprehensive landscape services across Westborough and Central Massachusetts—crafted for durability and long-term value.

Seasonal Maintenance & Stewardship

Recurring mowing, mulching, seasonal cleanups, and programmatic inspections to protect landscape value.

Landscape Design & Consultation

Practical site plans and plant palettes focused on durability, low maintenance, and curb appeal.

Hardscape & Paving

Repair and installation of patios, walkways, and steps with professional site preparation for durable results.

Stone Masonry & Foundations

Structural stonework and foundation preparation for durable, beautiful projects that stand the test of time.

Planting & Establishment

Specimen and mass plantings with native plant selection and soil preparation to ensure long-term vigor.

Hedge Sculpting & Privacy Screens

Precision pruning and shaping for living privacy walls and formal hedges that offer year-round screening.

Native Plant & Pollinator Gardens

Low-maintenance native beds that improve biodiversity and reduce ongoing upkeep while supporting pollinators.

Irrigation Systems & Drainage

Installation and repair of irrigation, smart controllers, and drainage solutions like French drains to protect your site.

Tree & Shrub Care & Pruning

Structural pruning, hazard mitigation, and seasonal health assessments to maintain healthy woody plants.

Lawn Care & Turf Management

Aeration, overseeding, and turf health assessments to keep lawn areas resilient and attractive.

Erosion Control & Grading

Grading corrections, swales, and soil stabilization to prevent runoff and protect your property assets.

Landscape Lighting & Outdoor Living

Low-voltage lighting and outdoor features to extend your property's usability and beauty after dark.
